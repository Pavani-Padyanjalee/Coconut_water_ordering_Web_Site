Who We Are...



Coconari is renowned for manufacturing and supplying fresh and organic coconut water in Sri Lanka. We hold a great standard for manufacturing best coconut water products among the competitiors.

Organic farming also promotes the preservation of topsoil, which is crucial for food production, but is eroded on a large scale of each year because of the human interference.

The farmers who encouraged by our Coconari company are working with harmony with the nature by means of sustainable, NON GMO farming with no chemicals.





Vision

          To become the preferred supplier and the largest organic coconut water supplier in the world with the reputation for quality, safety and the capacity.

Mission

          Produce & provide high quality coconut water products that are manufactured protecting the nutritional compositions and with zero additives.



What Do We Do...


We are involved in the full life cycle of our production process from encouraging the farmers,
collecting to manufacturing and supplying.



1) Collecting Coconuts...

Every coconut that turned into our products is hand-picked at its peak for optimal freshness and nutritional value and processed & manufactured using the ultra modern technologies at our own factory premises.

2) Processing....

Peeling off the green husk to remove soil and reduce microorganism contaminated on the surface. To further prevent browning and mold growth on the husk surface, coconuts are dipped in solution.


3) Manufacturing...

Using the state of art manufacturing machineries, the raw coconuts are crushed, filter, or other necessary steps are taken under the scrunity of the trained personals. there is a minimum human involvement and quality and safet is check each step of the manufacturing process.

4) Quality Checking...

Verification of the chemicals and microorganisms contamination in the coconut water and coconut meat such as agriculture chemicals, heavy metals bacteria in the modern laboratory.


5) Bottleing...

Then the processed products are bottled in standard reusable plastic bottles and are been stored in the chilling rooms before being subjected to structured quality control procedures.

6) Delivering...

Finally, our delivery team is ready to deliver the orders islandwide.
